Find Bad Words In Your Puzzle

Our Word Search Maker randomly places letters around your words to hide them. On ocassion, this could result in a random bad word showing up in the puzzle. If one is found that you feel could be harmful to your target audience, we suggest that you regenrate the puzzle by pressing F5, or clicking your browsers "reload page" button. Alternatively, advanced users can note the location of the bad words using the image below - then close this message, and edit the puzzle letters manually by putting your cursor next to it and hit 'backspace', then replace with a different letter.
